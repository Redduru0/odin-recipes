Thomas the Train Cake Recipe

Description

This cake serves 40 people , you can scale down if you have less guests.

Ingredients

  • 400g black fondant
  • 650g blue fondant
  • 450g red fondant
  • 30g yellow fondant
  • 160g grey fondant

Steps:

  1. Make the fondant details ahead of time so they can dry out
  2. Bake the cakes, prepare the frosting and make the simple syrup 2 days before the party
  3. Construct and decorate the cake the day before the party. See the video for demonstration You'll need a very strong cake board that is at least 35cm (13.78 inches) long and 20cm (7.87 inches) wide
  4. If it's blazing where you live, store in fridge, otherwise room temperature is fine
